Monstroriam is a mod focused on adding new magic and summon itens, mostly pre-hardmode.

Features:
2 Magic Weapons
8 Summon Weapons
3 Sentry Weapons
3 Accessories
3 "Arena" Furnitures

PT-BR Localization
